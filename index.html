Simple Present Tense

Main Formula
Subject + Base Form of Verb (+ s/es for 3rd person singular)
‚úÖ AFFIRMATIVE
Subject + V1 + Object

Examples:

  • She works in a bank.
  • They live in London.
  • We study English.
  • He reads books daily.
